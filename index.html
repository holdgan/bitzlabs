Polkadot

Polkadot is an open-source sharding multichain protocol that facilitates the cross-chain transfer of any data or asset types, thereby making a wide range of blockchains interoperable with each other.


NEAR Protocol

NEAR Protocol is a decentralized application platform designed to make apps usable on the web.


Filecoin

Filecoin is a decentralized storage system that aims to “store humanity’s most important information.”


Oasis Network

Designed for the next generation of blockchain, the Oasis Network is the first privacy-enabled blockchain platform for open finance and a responsible data economy.


Wootrade

Wootrade is a layer one trading infrastructure complete with deep liquidity, frontend trading GUI, and the ability to integrate into any exchange, trading desk, wallet, dApp, or other trading-related platform.


NuCypher

NuCypher is a decentralized encryption, access control and key management system (KMS), encryption service for publicblockchains.
